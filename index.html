What is The Secret Garden?

The Secret Garden Global (TSG), an interdenominational ministry with a global reach. TSG's mandate is to equip believers to walk in true dominion and to make real disciples of Christ. This vision is expressed through key programs such as the Secret Garden Retreat, Flames, Power Night, and TSG Missions, which takes place across multiple locations including Nigeria, the United Kingdom, Ethiopia, the United States, Kenya, Uganda, and many other regions at various times throughout the year.

Known as The Love Team, The Secret Garden Global is committed to nurturing the spiritual growth of believers, empowering them to live as God's ambassadors and to fulfil the One Billion Souls mandate by bringing spiritual restoration to the lost.
