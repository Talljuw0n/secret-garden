About the Event

A Sacred Gathering

What is The Secret Garden?

The Secret Garden Global (TSG), an interdenominational ministry with a global reach. TSG's mandate is to equip believers to walk in true dominion and to make real disciples of Christ. This vision is expressed through key programs such as the Secret Garden Retreat, Flames, Power Night, and TSG Missions, which takes place across multiple locations including Nigeria, the United Kingdom, Ethiopia, the United States, Kenya, Uganda, and many other regions at various times throughout the year. Known as The Love Team, The Secret Garden Global is committed to nurturing the spiritual growth of believers, empowering them to live as God's ambassadors and to fulfil the One Billion Souls mandate by bringing spiritual restoration to the lost.

Purpose of the Gathering

The Secret Garden exists to cultivate women who know how to:

  • War in prayer
  • Stand in conviction
  • Lead with wisdom
  • Carry God's presence with strength and grace

We create environments where women are not entertained but they are equipped.

  • Where weakness is strengthened
  • Where identity is restored
  • Where spiritual authority is activated

Who Should Attend?

  • Believers seeking spiritual renewal and deeper faith
  • Ministers and church leaders looking for fresh inspiration
  • Youth and young adults passionate about their faith journey
  • Families desiring to grow together in spiritual unity
  • Anyone hungry for a genuine encounter with God

What to Expect

  • Discipline in the secret place
  • Spiritual intelligence
  • Emotional healing and wholeness
  • Kingdom alignment
  • Expansion of capacity
